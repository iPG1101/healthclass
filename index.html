NUTRIENTS OF THE TOMATO

Tomatoes are a great source of antioxidant lycopene, which help reduce heart attacks and cancer.

They also are a good source of vitamin C, which helps form blood cells, muscle, bones, et cetera.

NUTRIENTS OF THE TOMATO

Tomatoes are a nice source of potassium as well, and that helps maintain a constant blood pressure.
